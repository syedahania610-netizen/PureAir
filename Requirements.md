Requirements

Overview
PureAir is an AI based application that shows real time air quality index of a user location and provides personalized health recommendations.
It is mainly designed for asthma patients and pollution sensitive users.

Problem Statement
Most AQI applications only show numbers.
Users do not understand what actions to take especially people with health issues like asthma.

Objectives
Provide easy to understand AQI information
Give personalized health advice
Help users decide whether to go outside or stay indoors

Target Users
Asthma patients
People with breathing problems
Elderly users
General public

Functional Requirements
Detect user location
Fetch real time AQI data
Allow user to enter health conditions
Generate personalized recommendations
Provide AI chatbot interaction

Non Functional Requirements
Fast response time
Simple and user friendly interface
Secure handling of user data

Constraints
Dependent on third party AQI APIs
Internet connection required

Future Scope
Regional language support
AQI alerts and notifications
Advanced AI based health predictions
